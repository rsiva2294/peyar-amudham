### 🏺 Full Description
> **Explore the linguistic heritage of Tamil names.**
> 
> Peyar Amudham is a digital archive featuring over 31,000 classical Tamil names curated from ancient roots. This application is designed for those seeking names that are authentic and historically grounded.
> 
> **Core Features:**
> 
> * **Extensive Archive**: Browse a curated list of traditional names cross-verified for linguistic authenticity.
> * **Vibe Search**: Use search technology to find names based on a description or a feeling (e.g., "brave warrior" or "soft light").
> * **Precision Filtering**: Search by gender, name length, and starting letter. Supports searching in both English and Tamil scripts.
> * **Local Favorites**: Save your preferred names to a personal list stored securely on your device.
> * **Heritage Focused**: An interface designed to reflect the cultural aesthetics of Tamil history.
> * **Offline Access**: Browse and search the complete archive without requiring an internet connection.
> 
> **Linguistic Authenticity**
> Every name in this collection is selected based on classical roots, focusing on the beauty of the traditional Tamil language.
> 
> **Privacy**
> We do not collect personal information. The app does not require an account, and your favorite list remains private on your device.
> 
> Peyar Amudham is an independent initiative dedicated to preserving the phonetic beauty of classical Tamil names for the global community.
