Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 10, 2026

Welcome to Peyar Amudham. Your privacy is a priority for us. This policy explains how we handle your data when you use our app and website.

1. Data We Do Not Collect

Peyar Amudham is designed to be a private tool. We do not collect:

  • Personally Identifiable Information (PII) like names, emails, or phone numbers.
  • Your location or device IDs.
  • Account or profile information (the app does not require login).

2. Local Storage

To provide features like "Favorites," we store specific name IDs locally on your device's storage. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible by us.

3. Artificial Intelligence (Vibe Search)

When you use the "Vibe Search" feature, the text you type is sent to Google Gemini AI to find matching linguistic patterns. Google's use of this data is governed by their Privacy Policy. We do not store your search queries.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following services to deliver Peyar Amudham:

  • Firebase Hosting: To deliver the web application. Firebase may collect basic server logs to ensure service reliability.
  • Google Play Store: To distribute the Android application. Their terms apply to your app installation.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Any changes will be reflected here with a new "Last Updated" date.

6. Contact Us

If you have questions about this policy, you can reach out via our GitHub repository.
